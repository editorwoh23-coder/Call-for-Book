📚 Call for Book Chapters

We invite submissions for our upcoming edited collection

About This Collection

We are pleased to announce a call for chapters for our upcoming edited book. This is an excellent opportunity for scholars, researchers, and practitioners to contribute to a significant publication that will advance knowledge in the field.

We welcome contributions from academics, professionals, and subject matter experts across all relevant disciplines.

Chapter Guidelines

  • Length: 6,000-8,000 words (excluding references)
  • Format: Microsoft Word (.docx) or PDF
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • References: Chicago Manual of Style or APA format
  • Figures/Tables: Include captions and source information

Submission Requirements

✓ Original Work

Chapters must be original and not previously published elsewhere

✓ Author Details

Include full name, affiliation, email, and 50-word bio

✓ Abstract

Provide a 150-word abstract of your chapter

Important Dates

Submissions Open
Accept chapter proposals and full submissions
Deadline
Final submission deadline for all chapters
Review
Peer review process (4-6 weeks)
Revisions
Authors submit revisions based on feedback
Publication
Book publication and launch

Review Process

All submissions will undergo a rigorous peer-review process to ensure quality and relevance. The review process includes:

  • Initial editorial screening for fit and quality
  • Blind peer review by 2-3 subject experts
  • Editor feedback and recommendations
  • Opportunity for authors to revise and resubmit
  • Final editorial acceptance
⚠️ Important: By submitting your chapter, you agree to our publishing terms and conditions. All accepted chapters will be published in the edited volume with proper attribution and copyright agreements will be established.

How to Submit

Please prepare your chapter according to the guidelines above and submit:

  • Your chapter manuscript
  • Abstract (150 words)
  • Author biography (50 words)
  • Complete author information (name, affiliation, email, phone)

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I submit a proposal first? Yes, you can submit a detailed proposal (500 words) before completing the full chapter.
  • What is the acceptance rate? We aim to accept approximately 70-80% of submitted chapters after peer review.
  • Will I receive feedback if rejected? Yes, constructive feedback will be provided to help improve future submissions.
  • Can I submit in multiple languages? All submissions must be in English.
  • Is there a publication fee? No, there are no author fees or publication charges.
